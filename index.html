Our Story

  • Mentorship app for Women Who Code
  • Fast track your career prospects
  • Peer learning and coaching made simple
  • Build an enduring professional network
We all know that getting the right advice at the right time and from the right people can dramatically change your life. In today's world, it can be daunting for anyone to navigate the career jungle. This is especially true for women in technology. The tech world can be a bit of a boys club. There are many women out there who are passionate about technology, who wants to advanced their careers in technology, and they need all the help they can get. One organization that is dedicated to promoting women in technology is Women Who Code. We created our app for Women Who Code. In creating this app, we had the following goals in mind. First, we would like to see more women fast track their careers prospects through mentoring. To make this happen, we want our app to make process of peer mentoring super simple. Lastly, we hope that using our app over time will allow more women to build enduring professional network.
